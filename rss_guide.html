Subscribing to RSS feed

Read time: <1 min

To ensure you get notified from sites you like, you must subscribe (or syndicate) to the letters they send.
Those letters they send is typically in a XML file, and typically, it's used for RSS feed.

What is RSS?

RSS is a shorthand term for Really Simple Syndication, or typically, RDF Site Summary.
It is used to syndicate, subscribe, and to pull content from a website, such as this website!

Where do I subscribe?

You can subscribe to most news sites, blogs that support RSS. You can search for RSS supported websites, or, if you find a RSS button on a website, that is too.
